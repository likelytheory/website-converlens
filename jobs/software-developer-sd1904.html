Background

Converlens is an Australian based company building an AI driven stakeholder engagement platform that gives people a voice and, more critically, government the tools to listen and understand what's being said. This means analysis, data exploration and reporting, providing tools to ask questions of data like "show me the key themes discussed by young women expressing highly negative sentiment and mentioning the Minister of Parliament".

On the back of successful proof of concept that ended in Oct 2018, we're now moving into the commercialisation phase - and are working with several federal government agencies in Australia, including the Department of Industry, Innovation and Science, the Department of the Prime Minister & Cabinet, and the Department of Education on some pretty cool projects (like the National Hydrogen Strategy and the A.I. Futures projects).

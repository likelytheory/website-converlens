Collect & Import. Manage & Organise. Analyse & Report.

We can help.

Converlens will help you manage your data end to end. Whether you are working with surveys, long or short form submissions or even printed documents, We can help you manage qualitative data all in one place.

Using another solution to capture data? No worries. We make it easy to upload data you have collected through other solutions so you can easily pull in​sights that are meaningful for your project.

Find out how Converlens can make collecting, managing and analysing data as easy as 123.

1 Collect and Import Data

  • Build powerful surveys

    Converlens allows you to build powerful, branded surveys instantly. Customise and edit as you need and explore insights in real-time as people submit their responses.

  • Easily import your existing data

    We support data exports from a wide variety of survey apps and data tools, so you can analyse all your data in one place.

  • Transcription for all data

    Data in a mess? We provide a full data transcription service to get your handwritten forms, scanned documents, and recorded audio into digital text, ready for analysis.

2 Manage and Organise

  • Label and code to organise everything

    Create your own structure to track and filter by semantic (e.g. climate), meta (e.g. important), functional (e.g. needs review) and structural (eg. legal-dept) labels and tags.

  • Edit, redact and publish

    Publish submissions with confidence. Edit and redact sensitive information, transcribe to web accessible format and publish submissions at the click of a button.

3 Analyse and Report

  • Powerful search tools for docs and data

    Dive deep into submissions and documents with advanced text matching, and a Related Terms search that finds “similar concepts” within your data.

  • Filter and query

    Ask real questions of your data and explore the underlying connections, using filters, categories and codes to examine exactly those segments you're interested in.

  • Themes, key phrases, quotes, context

    Gain rapid insights into critical topics and concerns being expressed. Converlens AI powered natural language tools will help shape your analysis and give you a feel for what's being said.

  • Analyse, visualise and report

    Our visualisation tools will help you see qualitative data in a new way, with clusters and groupings of the discussion, sentiment changes, and cross-data analysis toolsets.

    Take reporting of quantitative data to a new level, by instantly creating charts of your demographic and categorical data.
